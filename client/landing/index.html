A Little About Our Past

Founded in August of 2015 and based in Honolulu, Hawaii. AACU is a trusted community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book the sports leading professionals located around the world-online or from a mobile phone.

Whether a gym would like to book an instructor for a day, choreographer for a week, or a team coach for a month, AACU globally connects the cheerleading community so that teams from all over the world have equal resources. With world-class customer service and a growing community of gyms and professionals, AACU is the easiest and safest way for people gyms to book professionals, while professionals the opportunity to showcase their talents to a world-wide audience.

A Typical Communications

Client meet Specialist

  • Client

    Hello, I am looking for a skills instructor to help my Senior 3 and Open 3 teams with their tumbling and stunting technique during the dates that i have requested. We are a new club and are trying to push our level 2 teams to start learning level 3 skills. There are 20 athletes on each of the teams, all of which are new to level 3.

  • Specialist

    Hi, thank you for your booking! That won’t be a problem at all. How long would you like me to work with each team per day during the 2 days you have requested?

  • Client

    We have told the teams that they will have practice on both Saturday and Sunday from 10-1 and 2-5 each day. All of the girls have standing BHS and running 3 BHS as well as half-ups and straight up extensions. Therefore, we would like them to now learn proper tuck technique, full-ups, switch-ups, and any other at level skills you feel they are ready to attempt. What are your rates for the 6 hours you will be working with my team?

  • Specialist

    Great! I am big on teaching the proper progressions before moving on to new skills also. Moreover, if I feel that I need to work longer with the athletes I will be sure to let you know. Therefore, if you could let the pre ya know that the clinics may run at least a hour later that would be greatly appreciated. My hourly rate for clinics is $100 USD per hour, thus making your total $1,200 USD. All of my other rates for private/group lessons etc, are also listed on my profile if you are interested as well.

  • Client

    Will you be willing to offer a discounted rate being that we are offering you two teams instead of just one!?

  • Specialist

    Yes, I'll agree to $1,000 USD provided we keep to the same 12 hour schedule and private/group lessons are available as well.

  • Client

    Fantastic, no problem. Thank you! I will contact AACU and secure our booking, flight and your accommodations. Once everything is booked through them I will send you your itinerary of what time your flight will be, who will be picking you up from the airport, where you will be staying, etc. We will most likely book your flight the day before and have you leave after practice on Sunday so that you will be able to get back to your normal schedule. Looking forward to having you out!

  • Specialist

    Sounds great, and thank you again for your booking. Looking forward to working with you, see you soon!

About Us

Gunung Rinjani adalah gunung yang berlokasi di Pulau Lombok, Nusa Tenggara Barat. Gunung ini merupakan gunung favorit bagi pendaki Indonesia karena keindahan pemandangannya. Gunung ini merupakan bagian dari Taman Nasional Gunung Rinjani yang memiliki luas sekitar 41.330 ha dan ini akan diusulkan penambahannya sehingga menjadi 76.000 ha ke arah barat dan timur. Secara administratif gunung ini berada dalam wilayah tiga kabupaten: Lombok Timur, Lombok Tengah dan Lombok Barat.
